About Us

We are a team of developers and engineers passionate about using technology to improve public safety. Our work focuses on blending artificial intelligence and web technologies to create real-time detection systems that can be easily used in everyday environments.

What We Do?


We develop a real-time face mask detection system that uses computer vision and machine learning to identify whether a person is wearing a mask. Through a simple web interface built with Flask, users can access live webcam detection and see results instantly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support community health and safety by providing intelligent, accessible, and efficient AI solutions. We aim to make real-time detection tools that are accurate, lightweight, and suitable for real-world use in schools, workplaces, and public areas.

Why Choose Us?

Our system stands out for its simplicity, accuracy, and speed. It runs directly in a web browser, \ equires minimal setup, and is backed by a well-trained detection model. We focus on creating practical, user-friendly applications that make AI technology approachable for everyone.
